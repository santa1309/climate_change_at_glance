    About the Climate Anomaly Atlas

    An interactive atlas of weekly rainfall and temperature anomalies for every district in India, built from IMD daily gridded data.

    • Rainfall — deviation from the 1961–2010 normal (%).
    • Max / Min temperature — anomaly from the 2016–2024 normal (°C).

    Pick a year / month / week, switch the active layer, and click any district for its actual vs. normal values and a weekly trajectory chart. District values are area-weighted zonal means.
